Corn Flaking Grits

CORN FLAKING GRITS / MAIZE BROKEN :

Invariably known as Cracked Corn, Crushed Maize, MakkaTukadi (Tukari), Corn Grits,Corn Meal, Broken Corn.

1. CORN BROKEN JC001


These are the prime products from a full degerming system consisting of the largest pieces of broken maize endosperm free of husk and germ. Main uses are Cornflakes, It also is used for making "Tortillas" or a sort of porridge, Direct cooking and consumption (known as Samp in southern Africa)

Technical specification

4.5mm to 6 mm

2. CORN BROKEN JC002

Brewer's Grits - Slightly finer than cereal grits, used as a brewing adjunct and in malted beverages. This grits is also used for extruded snacks

Technical specification

4mm to 5 mm

3. CORN BROKEN JC003

This grits is used as a raw material for extruded snacks. It is also used to make finer corn meal. Apart from food industry, it serves as best ready-to-serve for birds, animals likes horses, goats, etc.

Technical specification

: 3 mm to 4 mm

4. CORN BROKEN JC004

This grits is also used for extruded snacks. Also this grade of Crushed Maize is used as Chick Feed, as it serves as ready to feed to the chicks.

Technical specification

2 mm to 3 mm
